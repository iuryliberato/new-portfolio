About Me

I’m Iury, a Web Developer based in London. Having previously worked in the hospitality industry for seven years, where I learned to be a valuable team member and solve problems under pressure, I felt confident and ready for a career change.

After teaching myself some coding online, I created my own personal website which allowed me to discover my passion for Web Development. To prepare for my first job as a Web Developer, I joined an immersive software engineering bootcamp with General Assembly.

I found love in coding because it allows me to be creative, curious and constantly learning new skills. After 10 years away from being in a classroom, learning how to code made me excited about how many things I’m capable of achieving.

Once I finished the course, I was offered a position as a Teaching Assistant. After working for General Assembly for 2 years, I’m now looking for my first role as a Web Developer.
